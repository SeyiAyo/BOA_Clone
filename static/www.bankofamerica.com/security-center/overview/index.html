Help avoid mail fraud this holiday season

Mail fraud is on the rise, and it can cause your money, or even your identity, to be stolen. Following a few simple steps can help keep you safe this holiday season.

To protect against mail fraud:

  • Try to avoid using blue mailboxes. Instead, hand mail directly to your postal carrier or go inside the post office. If you must use a blue mailbox, deposit mail as close to the pickup time as possible so it doesn't sit there for an extended period of time.
  • Use certified mail or a secure mailbox or go inside the post office when sending something of value. Confirm the receipt with your recipient.
  • Do not send cash or checks in the mail. Consider using digital payment methods to send money instead. To learn about or enroll in digital payment methods, visit our Security Center.
